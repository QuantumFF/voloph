# Precision-rebalanced segmentation: serve-confirmed default timeline, suppressed tier

Three measurement rounds on the v5 residuals (#93, plus #92's FP anatomy and #94's boundary provenance) closed every direction the current signal space offered: velocity re-keying, detector rate, sub-threshold admission, sample persistence, and span-integrated vision all hit the same monotone rally-vs-chatter trade far from ADR 0015's bar, while v5's false positives (58.3/h against a ≤ 5–10/h spec) are vision-indistinguishable from gold on every measured feature — two real people really moving on court. Audio onset density is the one measured separator (gold 1.08 / chatter 0.40 / FP spans 0.35 onsets/s median), and no FP span contains a serve. Round 3's pre-declared fallback — the spec-level conversation — was held 2026-07-12 and decided this ADR. Issue #95 carries the evidence pack and the gated next step.

**The acceptance bar flips from recall-first to precision-first on the default timeline — by relocating the zero-miss principle, not repealing it.** ADR 0015's "no precision is ever bought by paying recall" is superseded. The timeline gains a third tier, the **Suppressed span** (glossary in CONTEXT.md): a draft span the segmenter judged non-play, off the default view, revealed on demand, restorable with one operation, never destroyed. The bar becomes: default timeline ≤ 5–10 FP/h and edges ≲ 1 s; wrongly suppressed rallies ≤ ~20 of the 319-rally gold corpus; **hard misses — play absent even from the suppressed tier — ≈ 0**. The deepest ADR 0015 principle survives in narrowed form: no single signal ever *destroys* play, because suppression is not deletion. Rejected: deleting unconfirmed spans outright (rebuilds ADR 0006's silent audio-gate catastrophe with a camera — serve-cue blind spots are correlated by court side and occlusion, so failures would concentrate in single recordings, invisibly).

**v6 keeps v5's fusion shape and adds a confirmation stage: serve-cue OR strong-audio admits a proposal to the default timeline; both-miss suppresses it.** Occupancy still proposes (the one component with measured recall abundance), motion still refines edges. The **serve cue** is a nano-class, Apache-2.0 pose model (the popular Ultralytics pose models are AGPL) run only in densified windows (10–15 fps decode) around candidate span starts; a detected serve also snaps the span's start edge — attacking #94's start half. This is a **new signal class**, not the "bigger detector" ADR 0015 keeps closed: 0/22 residual misses trace to detector absence, so the nano detector stays. Rejected: serve-as-proposer (demands dense whole-recording pose compute, concentrates recall on one cue's correlated blind spots, and discards the proposer with measured recall); serve-only confirmation (spends its errors suppressing real rallies where the OR-rule spends them on rare hit-dense knock-ups surviving as one-`delete` FPs — the cheaper side of the new bar, and already an accepted ADR 0015 non-goal). **Audio may now co-confirm** — a narrowing of ADR 0015's "audio never gates": it still never solely deletes (suppression is recoverable, and serve provides the second path), its confirm bar moves from the measured-useless 0.2 onsets/s into the measured separating region (~0.5–0.8/s; exact value is a measurement output), and a per-recording poisoned-baseline guard (IMG_5933, neighbour-court bleed) degrades poisoned recordings to serve-only confirmation.

**Global `DETECT_FPS` stays 3.** Measured twice as a non-lever: misses double at unchanged knobs (the movement floor is rate-coupled and must be re-expressed per-second before any future raise buys anything). Densification is targeted and scratch-only until v6 ships.

**Everything is gated on a round-4 measurement with a pre-declared kill criterion** (#95): serve-cue recall at the 319 known gold starts per recording and court side, false-serve rates on matched chatter and v5's FP spans, the confirmation frontier, and the end-edge table (audio last-onset vs motion decay) that decides #94's end half. Green = an operating point suppressing ≥ 80% of v5's FP spans while wrongly suppressing ≤ 20/319 gold, inside the ADR 0015 compute envelope (≤ ~1.5× real-time CPU, GPU only accelerates). Red closes the posture direction like velocity and 5 fps before it. The corpus doubles as tuning data (five same-day recordings, two players' serves), so interpretable hand-constructed cues on declared grids only; fitted anything is advisory with leave-one-recording-out numbers.

**Versioning:** the built design is `SEGMENTER_VERSION` 6 (materially alters the draft — the ADR 0013/0015 bump trigger); the Analysis format grows a per-span tier. Staleness rules unchanged: hand-corrected timelines are never clobbered; untouched recordings re-analyze silently.

Supersedes ADR 0015's acceptance-bar paragraph and narrows its audio clause as stated; its constraint envelope (local, CPU-capable, GPU-optional, geometry-free, staticness, replaceable-behind-the-timeline, harness-as-referee, staleness rules) and ADR 0016's proposal mechanism all still hold.
