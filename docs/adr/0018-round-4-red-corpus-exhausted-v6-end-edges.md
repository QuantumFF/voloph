# Round-4 RED: confirmation mechanism struck, corpus exhausted for admission signals, v6 reassigned to end edges

The round-4 measurement that gated ADR 0017's v6 build (#95) came back **RED** against its pre-declared kill criterion: no point on the serve×audio grid reaches 80% FP suppression (max 78.8%, costing 218/319 gold rallies); inside the ≤20-gold budget the frontier suppresses only 22% of v5's FP spans. The failure is physical, not parametric — interpretable elevation cues measure *arm raised*, which every overhead stroke satisfies, and the far court is a structural nano-pose blind spot (2/319 usable far-court poses). The judgment session of 2026-07-12 accepted the reading and decided the following.

**The interpretable-posture direction is closed** — like velocity re-keying and 5 fps before it, permanently on this corpus and camera geometry. No cue-grid extensions; if pose ever returns it is a different signal class, and only under the corpus condition below.

**ADR 0017's serve∨audio confirmation stage is struck; its spec stands without a mechanism.** The precision-first bar (default timeline ≤ 5–10 FP/h, edges ≲ 1 s, wrongly suppressed ≤ ~20/319, hard misses ≈ 0), the suppressed-span tier, and the two-level miss budget remain the acceptance spec for any future admission change — they were decided on product grounds round 4 did not touch. Rejected: reverting to ADR 0015's recall-first bar (v5 already satisfies it and is the thing that isn't good enough); weakening the bar to what audio alone buys today (58.3 → ~46 FP/h for 20 gold rallies — the frontier-moves-but-doesn't-reach shape RED exists to catch). Consequence stated plainly: an admission-changing v6 has no timeline.

**The 21-06-2026 gold corpus is declared exhausted for admission-signal discovery.** Four rounds of pre-declared criteria have uniformly closed every direction the corpus's signal space offers; the ceiling is the corpus's information content (five same-day recordings, two players), not the cue vocabulary. No round 5 against it — neither hand-constructed nor fitted. Corpus extension (#78) now gates any future admission round; when it lands, fitted approaches open with leave-one-recording-out validation, per ADR 0017's integrity rules.

**`SEGMENTER_VERSION` 6 is reassigned to the round's one clean win: end-edge placement by motion-envelope decay** (0.34 s median error at gold ends vs 1.46 s for audio last-onset, winning on all five recordings and clearing the ~1 s bar — settling #94's end half). Admission is untouched and the suppressed tier is not introduced (nothing measured to suppress with). The same bump fixes the latent `probe_dimensions` parse bug (trailing-separator ffprobe output silently falling back to a 416×416 square-frame assumption in occupancy box back-mapping), previously frozen only to preserve v5's byte-identical output — one re-analysis wave instead of two. Start edges are unchanged: serve-snap died with the confirmation stage and round 4 produced no start-edge evidence.

Supersedes ADR 0017's confirmation-stage and versioning paragraphs as stated; everything else in ADR 0017 and the ADR 0015/0016 envelope still holds.
